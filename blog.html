How Christ Glory Impacts Lives and Inspires Faith

At the core of Christ Glory's mission is a commitment to fostering spiritual growth and nurturing faith. The church offers a range of worship services that cater to the diverse needs of its congregation. From traditional hymns to contemporary worship, Christ Glory creates an atmosphere where people can connect with God in ways that resonate with their hearts.


The church's emphasis on spiritual growth is not limited to its Sunday services. It provides Bible study groups, mentorship programs, and various educational resources to help members deepen their understanding of their faith. These opportunities empower individuals to grow in their relationship with God and live out their beliefs.


Another vital aspect of Christ Glory's identity is its dedication to compassion and service. The church actively engages in outreach programs, volunteer efforts, and support for those in need within the local community. Whether it's feeding the hungry, assisting the homeless, or providing emotional support to those facing challenges, Christ Glory is deeply involved in making a positive impact on the lives of those it serves.


Christ Glory is a shining example of how a church can be the heart of a community. It provides a place of worship, spiritual growth, inclusivity, compassion, fellowship, and innovation. It serves as a source of hope and inspiration, touching the lives of its members and the broader community. This church demonstrates that the impact of faith goes beyond the sanctuary; it's a force for positive change in the world.


Sunday Service

Join us this and every Sunday at 6 Red Okai St, Accra at 9:00 AM for a refreshing time of corporate fellowship, worship and the word.

Also join us for a heartwarming evening of fellowship and worship at our annual "Harvest of Hope" event, with the due date being communicated soon. This special gathering, set to take place in the serene ambiance of our church, offers a time to come together as a community, express gratitude, and celebrate the abundant blessings of the season. We'll be treated to soul-stirring hymns, inspiring sermons, and a shared meal that will fill both our stomachs and spirits. All are welcome to attend, and we look forward to sharing this memorable evening with you.
