Our core values

Dispensing living words, forming Christ in men.

Our Mission

At Christ Glory, our mission is to be a beacon of God's love, a source of spiritual growth, and a force for positive change in our community and beyond. We are committed to fulfilling this mission through service, outreach, fellowship and prayer.

Our Vision

We envision a vibrant and welcoming community of believers, guided by faith, love, and a deep sense of purpose. Our vision is to create a place where all are invited to experience the transforming power of God's love and grace.

Our Purpose

The purpose of Christ Glory is to be a passionate and purposeful community of faith, dedicated to worshiping God, nurturing disciples, sharing the Gospel, caring for one another, serving the community, and growing in unity and diversity.

A place of worship and spiritual growth

Christ Glory is a vibrant and welcoming community of faith, located in the heart of Lapaz. Rooted in a rich history and guided by a compelling vision for the future, we are dedicated to serving as a place of spiritual nourishment, connection, and transformation.


We are a community of believers who come together to worship God with joy, reverence, and authenticity. Our worship services are designed to create an environment where people can encounter the presence of God and deepen their spiritual journey.
